Keeping Your Instagram Account Secure:

Password Requirements:

  • Include a mixture of upper and lowercase letters: combining both capital and small letters makes it harder for hackers to guess your password.
  • Include numbers: adding numbers to your password makes it significantly harder for hackers to guess.
  • Make sure your password is 8 characters or longer: longer passwords are much more secure.
  • Don’t use the same password for different accounts: using unique passwords for each of your accounts limits someone’s ability to gain access to multiple accounts if they were to somehow gain access to one.
  • Don’t use common words or phrases: using words that can easily be guessed make it much easier for hackers to gain access to your account.
